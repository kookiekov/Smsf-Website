Transistors!

Advantages and disadvantages of transistors.

The transistor is very important, but just like everything it is not perfect and it has some disadvantages. A few of them are:

Advantages Disadvantages
Low Source Voltage Temperature Dependency
High voltage gain Lower power dissipation
Smaller in size Low input impedance

However there are ways to overcome these troubles and the transistor is still arguably one of the most useful component seen on the boards.
